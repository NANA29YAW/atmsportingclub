About Us

Our noble football club was established in 2011, with the motive of grooming talented individuals to help them become better in their field of football.

U-10 Team

Our under 10 team involves young talented footballers who are in the early stages of their careers. We provide all the nurturing needed to develop their skills and help them progress to the next level.

U-13 Team

Our under 13 team comprises more experienced players who are ready to take their skills to the next level. Our coaches provide them with top-quality training and guidance to help them achieve their goals.

U-15 Team

Our under 15 team is focused on developing players with intermediate-level skills, preparing them to compete at higher levels with discipline and dedication.

U-17 Team

Our under 17 team features advanced players who are ready to showcase their talent on larger platforms. They receive tailored training to refine their abilities and achieve their dreams in football.
